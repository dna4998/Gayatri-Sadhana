🕉️ Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 13, 2026

Gayatri Sadhana ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Usage Data: App interactions, features used, and session duration to improve the experience.
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and app version for technical support.
  • Location Data: Approximate location (if permitted) to calculate Sandhya times for your timezone.
  • Practice Data: Your japa counts and meditation sessions, stored locally on your device.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve app features
  • To calculate accurate Sandhya prayer times based on your location
  • To send optional reminders and notifications (with your permission)
  • To analyze usage patterns and fix bugs

3. Data Storage

Your personal practice data (japa counts, meditation logs) is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers. We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties.

4. Third-Party Services

We may use the following third-party services:

  • Apple App Store: Subject to Apple's Privacy Policy
  • Analytics: Anonymized usage data only

5. Children's Privacy

Gayatri Sadhana is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete any personal data we hold about you. Contact us at support@deepdivemd.com to make any such request.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of changes by updating the date at the top of this page.

8. Contact Us
